# FLUID MECHANICS

## Pressure
*   **Absolute Pressure:**
    *   $P_{abs} = P_{atm} + P_{gage}$
    *   $P_{abs} = P_{atm} - P_{vac}$
*   **Pascal's Law (Hydraulic Jack):**
    *   $\frac{F_1}{A_1} = \frac{F_2}{A_2} \rightarrow \frac{F_1}{d_1^2} = \frac{F_2}{d_2^2}$

## Continuity and Energy
*   **Flow Rate:** $Q = A \times v$
*   **Mass Flow Rate:** $\dot{m} = \rho Av$
*   **Continuity Equation:**
    *   $A_1v_1 = A_2v_2$
    *   $d_1^2v_1 = d_2^2v_2$
*   **Bernoulli's Equation:**
    *   $E_T = \frac{v^2}{2g_c^*} + \frac{Zg}{g_c^*} + \frac{P}{\rho}$
    *   $H_T = \frac{v^2}{2g} + Z + \frac{P}{\gamma}$
    *   *Note: Eng: (*) = 778 and SI: (*) = 1000*
*   **Pumps & Turbines:**
    *   **Pumps:** $P = \frac{\gamma QH}{\eta_p}$
    *   **Turbines:** $P = \gamma \eta_T Q$

## Sound and Fluids
*   **Mach Number:** $Ma = \frac{v_{gas}}{v_{sound}}$
*   **Bulk Modulus:** $K = \frac{\Delta P}{\Delta V / V_o}$
*   **Sound Velocity:** $v_{sound} = \sqrt{\frac{Kg_c}{\rho}}$

## Jets & Orifices
*   **Orifice & Jet Flow:**
    *   $C_D = \frac{Q}{A_n \sqrt{2gH}} = \frac{v_{actual}}{\sqrt{2gH}}$
    *   **Time to drain tank:** $t = \frac{2A_{tank}(\sqrt{h_1} - \sqrt{h_2})}{C_D A_n \sqrt{2g}}$
    *   **Force of jet:** $F_{jet} = \rho A_n v^2$
